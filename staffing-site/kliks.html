What Is Kliks?

Kliks is an advanced AI-powered helpdesk and real-time support platform designed to transform the way organizations deliver technical assistance and customer support. The platform combines artificial intelligence, live agent expertise, and real-time video conferencing to provide a seamless and highly efficient support experience.

Kliks leverages AI-driven triage and intelligent routing to connect users with the most qualified support agents instantly — reducing resolution time and improving satisfaction.
