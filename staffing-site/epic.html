We Know Epic Inside and Out

Data, delivery, strategy, and long-term support. Whatever stage you're at, we've probably been there a few hundred times.

Data Management & Integration

Getting your data right is the hardest part. We actually like doing it.

Most Epic projects don't fail on the clinical side. They stall because somebody underestimated the data work. Migration mapping, interface testing, warehouse alignment -- it's tedious, high-stakes, and we happen to be very good at it. We've moved terabytes of legacy data into Epic without losing a single patient record, and we've built HL7/FHIR integrations that IT teams actually trust.

Data Migration

We've migrated data from every major legacy EHR into Epic -- Cerner, MEDITECH, Allscripts, you name it. Our process covers validation, mapping, and QA so you don't discover data gaps the week before go-live. Every migration gets a reconciliation report your CMO can actually read.

HL7, FHIR & API Integration

Labs, pharmacies, imaging, third-party apps -- if it needs to talk to Epic, we'll build the interface and make sure it stays up.

Clarity/Caboodle & EDW

Your data warehouse is only useful if people use it. We build Clarity and Caboodle reports that answer the questions your ops and clinical teams are actually asking -- not the ones that looked good in the original spec.

Clinical Data Abstraction

Accurate extraction for quality reporting and regulatory compliance.

Project Delivery

We've been through enough go-lives to know what actually matters on launch day. And the six months after.

Implementation & Go-Live

Build, test, validate, go-live, stabilize. We staff every phase -- including the command center at 2 AM on Day 1, and the at-the-elbow support that keeps clinicians from revolting on Day 3. We've done this across community hospitals, academic medical centers, and multi-facility health systems.

Project & Program Management

Our PMs don't just track Gantt charts. They know Epic well enough to push back on bad timelines and escalate before things slip.

Workflow Redesign

The fastest way to blow up an Epic rollout is to automate a bad workflow. We sit with clinicians and ops teams to redesign before we build, so you're not just digitizing problems. This usually saves more money than the technology itself.

Operational & Clinical Excellence

Measurable gains in throughput, quality metrics, and safety outcomes -- not just a nicer-looking dashboard.

Advisory Services

Your Epic roadmap shouldn't live in a slide deck nobody reads.

We help CIOs and IT leadership turn Epic strategy into something that actually drives decisions. That means honest assessments of where you are, a realistic plan for where you're headed, and the governance to keep everyone accountable along the way. No 200-page reports that collect dust.

Roadmap Planning

Digital Transformation

Epic as the foundation for telehealth, patient engagement, and emerging tech. Practical, not buzzwordy.

Change Management

Adoption programs that actually move the needle. We've learned that the hardest part of any Epic project isn't technical -- it's getting 4,000 clinicians to change how they work. Our change management approach focuses on physician champions, department-level training plans, and feedback loops that start before go-live and don't stop after.

App Rationalization

Governance & PMO
